Stainless Steel Closed Head Drum

Sizes available in : 5L, 12L, 20L, 28L, 40L, 60L, 75L, 110L & 210L

  • UN Approved : 1A1 / X1.6 / 250 / IND

  • Internal Finish : 2B / BA (Mirror)
